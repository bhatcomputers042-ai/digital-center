How to use

  1. Upload a document image.
  2. Click Auto Detect Document.
  3. If needed, drag the 4 corner points to match the page edges.
  4. Click Crop Selected Area.
  5. Enable black & white mode or adjust brightness/contrast if needed.
  6. Download the final scanned image.
